## What to send

Each kwarg becomes one OTel span attribute, which our ingest first-classes into a column. **What you don't pass becomes invisible to that column.** See [attribute mapping](/concepts/attribute-mapping) for the full table.

<Tip>
  Pass `provider`, `convo_id`, `user_id`, and `model` on every call. Skipping any of them silently disables a dashboard feature.
</Tip>
